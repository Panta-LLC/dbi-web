# Delta Bay Impact Website Personas

This document captures priority personas for the Delta Bay Impact (DBI) website.
It aligns to DBI's mission to educate, advocate, and elevate African American youth
and families in Contra Costa communities.

## Persona 1: Student Participant

- **Name (sample)**: Jamal, 13
- **Role**: Middle school student at Riverview
- **Context**: Often home alone after school; wants support and belonging
- **Primary Goals**
  - Feel safe and included at school
  - Get help with homework and test prep
  - Find mentors and role models who understand his culture
- **Needs**
  - Clear, inviting program descriptions
  - Easy way to join activities and events
  - Stories of students like him succeeding
- **Pain Points**
  - Feels isolated or "othered" at school
  - Unsure where to go for help
  - Limited family time due to parents' work schedules
- **Motivations**
  - Belonging, encouragement, and confidence
  - Fun, meaningful activities after school
- **Content That Helps**
  - Student spotlight stories and testimonials
  - Simple "How we help" summaries
  - Photos/videos of safe spaces and mentors
- **Key Messages**
  - "You belong here."
  - "We help you succeed in school and life."
- **Primary CTA**
  - "Join a DBI program"

## Persona 2: Parent or Guardian

- **Name (sample)**: Monique, 37
- **Role**: Working parent of two students
- **Context**: Long commute; wants trusted support for her children
- **Primary Goals**
  - Ensure children are safe and supported after school
  - Build confidence and academic success for her kids
  - Stay informed about school and community resources
- **Needs**
  - Trust signals: mission, values, and staff credibility
  - Clear program schedules and locations
  - Family engagement events and resources
- **Pain Points**
  - Limited time to research programs
  - Past experiences of schools not meeting her children's needs
- **Motivations**
  - A trusted "village" that supports her family
  - Culturally responsive mentorship for her children
- **Content That Helps**
  - Family engagement night details
  - FAQ on safety, schedules, and costs
  - Contact info and simple inquiry form
- **Key Messages**
  - "We partner with families to help students thrive."
  - "Safe spaces, caring mentors, and real results."
- **Primary CTA**
  - "Request program info"

## Persona 3: School Administrator or Educator

- **Name (sample)**: Mr. Rivera, 46
- **Role**: Assistant Principal at Olympic High
- **Context**: Seeks partners who improve engagement and belonging
- **Primary Goals**
  - Improve student engagement and attendance
  - Support culturally responsive mentorship
  - Strengthen family-school relationships
- **Needs**
  - Evidence of impact and outcomes
  - Clear partnership model and scope of services
  - Alignment with school priorities
- **Pain Points**
  - Limited resources and staff capacity
  - Difficulty reaching families consistently
- **Motivations**
  - Reliable, proven partner with community trust
- **Content That Helps**
  - Program outcomes and pilot results
  - Partner testimonials from other schools
  - Partnership overview and service menu
- **Key Messages**
  - "DBI bridges the gap between school and families."
  - "We provide mentorship, advocacy, and wrap-around supports."
- **Primary CTA**
  - "Start a school partnership"

## Persona 4: Donor, Foundation, or Community Partner

- **Name (sample)**: Elena, 52
- **Role**: Program Officer at a local foundation
- **Context**: Funding youth development and racial equity initiatives
- **Primary Goals**
  - Invest in measurable impact for African American youth
  - Support scalable, community-rooted programs
  - Fund organizations with clear sustainability plans
- **Needs**
  - Mission clarity and outcomes
  - Transparent funding needs and budget overview
  - Stories of student and family impact
- **Pain Points**
  - Many organizations claim impact without evidence
- **Motivations**
  - Long-term, sustainable change in education equity
- **Content That Helps**
  - Impact metrics and success stories
  - Sustainability plan highlights
  - Partner and supporter list
  - Volunteer or in-kind opportunities
- **Key Messages**
  - "Your support creates pathways to belonging and success."
  - "DBI is building sustainable, community-led change."
- **Primary CTA**
  - "Donate or partner"

## Content Priorities by Persona

- **Students**: Stories, photos, program benefits, easy sign-up
- **Parents/Guardians**: Trust, safety, schedules, contact, FAQs
- **School Leaders**: Outcomes, partnership model, references
- **Donors/Partners**: Impact data, budget, sustainability, ways to help

## Site-Wide Voice and Tone

- **Warm and affirming**: emphasize belonging and community
- **Clear and practical**: simple next steps and easy contact
- **Credible and transparent**: show outcomes, partners, and plans

## Core CTAs for Navigation

- "Join a DBI program"
- "Request program info"
- "Start a school partnership"
- "Donate or partner"
- "Contact DBI"
